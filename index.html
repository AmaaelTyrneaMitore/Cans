Order Cancellation Scenarios

  • Case 1: Pre-enroute Orders: Customer wants to cancel the order as they no longer want it/ they have ordered by mistake and order is not delayed OR order is delayed by less than 10 minutes:
  • We understand that you changed your mind/ bought the product by mistake.
  • We at Blinkit, strive to save our environment by utilizing the resources effectively. Since, the cancellation would always lead to the wastage of the food, items/products, packaging etc. It would be great if you could accept the order this time.
  • We at Blinkit, strive to preserve the environment and prevent waste, thus, we would be delighted if you consider accepting the order this time.
  • Case 2: Enroute Orders: Customer wants to cancel the order as they no longer want it/ they have ordered by mistake and order is not delayed OR order is delayed by less than 10 minutes:
  • We understand that you changed your mind/ bought the product by mistake and want the order to be cancelled.
  • However, our delivery partner has made a significant effort to ensure the delivery within the suggested timeframe, we would be grateful if you accept the order.
  • We understand it is very normal to have second thoughts, please give us a chance to get your request fulfilled as our delivery partner has made a significant effort and the order has already been processed and is about to be delivered to you.
  • Case 3: Customer wants to cancel the order due to an incorrect address/location:
  • Realizing that the address you provided was incorrect can be extremely disheartening. Would you please let us know if the correct location/delivery address is nearby?

If yes, please ask the customer to share the address and check with the DP to see if the order can be delivered.

If the answer is no, please inform the customer that we are cancelling the order.

  • Guide the customer with the future steps:

In cases, where you want to place the order for someone else, you can always follow the below steps:

  1. Turn off the location (if turned on)
  2. Select the location where you want the order to be delivered.
  3. Add the products to your shopping cart
  4. Enter the complete address (if it has been not saved) and click on “Place order”.
  • Case 4: The delivery partner called the customer stating that he is unable to deliver the order due to (Vehicle issues, accident, incorrect mapping, or extreme weather):
  • Validate the same with the DP/SM
  • We apologise for the situation that prevented the delivery partner from making the delivery at your location. Hence, we will be cancelling the order and will make sure no cancellation fees will be levied.
  • Case 5: Customer wants to cancel the order as they have forgotten to apply the Promo code/coupon in the particular order:

(OLD/EXISTING USERS)

  • It’s disappointing to miss out on a coupon but we want you to come back and know that you will be able to use it on your next order.

(FIRST ORDER)

  • Although you have forgotten to apply your first promo code, we have numerous other promo codes that you can make use of. We request that you check out our Offers page and you will be delighted about all the exciting offers.
  • Although it’s disappointing to miss out on a promo code, we want you to know that since it’s still day one, you will be able to apply other promo codes on your future orders.
  • Case 6: Customer wants to modify the order after placing ( Add or delete items from the order):

        If the customer wants to add:

  • I understand that you want to add items which you might have missed, however, a lot of effort goes into processing an order. Also, we would like to promote a safe environment with minimal wastage and the cancellation of an order will lead to wastage of resources. It would be humbling if you could accept this order and place a fresh order for the remaining items.

        If the customer wants to edit:

  • I understand that you have ordered a few extra items which were not required. Please understand a lot of effort goes into processing an order. Also, we would like to promote a safe environment with minimal wastage and the cancellation of an order will lead to wastage of resources.
  • You can always keep the extra items in stock for future use.

        If the customer is concerned about the delivery charges:

  • We acknowledge that while placing the next order you will incur delivery charges. However, we humbly request you to reconsider. These charges help ensure fair compensation for our delivery partners, who are hustling every minute to get orders delivered in these extreme conditions.

Wrong Item Delivered

Process

  • The customer raises a complaint stating that I have received an incorrect item and they’re okay to keep the item but request you refund the price difference:
  • Validate the images shared by the customer and if the brand name/product name is the same but the flavour/variant is different, offer Replacement in the first go, else process RPU.

Note: DO NOT OFFER  Re-delivery or  any partial refunds (price difference of the two items)

  • If RPU slots are not available/Items are Out of Stock:
  • (For Silver, Bronze and new Karma) If the item is ghee, oil, atta, rice, dal, or sugar ask the customer to raise the issue later. Other items - Process Item Refund
  • (For Gold) Process Complete item Refund (Disposition - Wrong Item)

  • I am connecting with the store with the feedback to ensure that this does not happen again in the future, and we hope to have the correct item delivered to you as soon as possible. If there is anything else we can do to make this right, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Missing Item

Process

  • Handling issue with partial items (For the cases where the customer orders a pack of two or a pack of multiple items or BOGO missing- and complain that few of the packets are missing- treat these complaints as Item Missing):
  • For eg: A customer orders a Pack of Maggi Masala (12 packets) and complains that I have received only 10 packets of Maggi Masala.
  • Disposition- Item Missing
  • Offer Replacement/RPU in the first go
  • Item is Out of Stock - RPU
  • If slots are not available then process a partial refund.
  • Please keep in mind that for Ghee, oil, atta, rice, dal, and sugar  (Silver, Bronze, New Karmas) product categories, replacement/ RPU is a must. No refunds are to be generated.

COD Disabled

  • Orders with a worth only greater than 100 and less than 1500 will qualify for COD for new users.
  • Orders for the remaining customers (aside from iron) that are only worth more than 50 and less than 3,000 are qualified for COD.
  • Other causes for debilitating COD, though, include:
  • There is a daily cap of two COD purchases.
  • The merchant's end could deactivate COD.

Account Deletion

  • I would like to inform you that if you delete your account you won't be able to retrieve it.

1. Log into the Blink app and tap the Settings icon at the bottom of the screen.

2. Tap on Account and Privacy.

3. On the Account and Privacy screen, select the "Delete Account" button.

4. Tap "Delete" on the popup confirmation.

5. Enter your account password to confirm the deletion of your account.
