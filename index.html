Order Cancellation Scenarios

  • Case 1: Pre-enroute Orders: Customer wants to cancel the order as they no longer want it/ they have ordered by mistake and order is not delayed OR order is delayed by less than 10 minutes:
  • We understand that you changed your mind/ bought the product by mistake.
  • We at Blinkit, strive to save our environment by utilizing the resources effectively. Since, the cancellation would always lead to the wastage of the food, items/products, packaging etc. It would be great if you could accept the order this time.
  • We at Blinkit, strive to preserve the environment and prevent waste, thus, we would be delighted if you consider accepting the order this time.
  • Case 2: Enroute Orders: Customer wants to cancel the order as they no longer want it/ they have ordered by mistake and order is not delayed OR order is delayed by less than 10 minutes:
  • We understand that you changed your mind/ bought the product by mistake and want the order to be cancelled.
  • However, our delivery partner has made a significant effort to ensure the delivery within the suggested timeframe, we would be grateful if you accept the order.
  • We understand it is very normal to have second thoughts, please give us a chance to get your request fulfilled as our delivery partner has made a significant effort and the order has already been processed and is about to be delivered to you.
  • Case 3: Customer wants to cancel the order due to an incorrect address/location:
  • Realizing that the address you provided was incorrect can be extremely disheartening. Would you please let us know if the correct location/delivery address is nearby?

If yes, please ask the customer to share the address and check with the DP to see if the order can be delivered.

If the answer is no, please inform the customer that we are cancelling the order.

  • Guide the customer with the future steps:

In cases, where you want to place the order for someone else, you can always follow the below steps:

  1. Turn off the location (if turned on)
  2. Select the location where you want the order to be delivered.
  3. Add the products to your shopping cart
  4. Enter the complete address (if it has been not saved) and click on “Place order”.

  • Validate the same with the DP/SM
  • We apologize for the situation that prevented the delivery partner from making the delivery at your location. Hence, we will be cancelling the order and will make sure no cancellation fees will be levied.
